# Launch Plan — AudioFile Premium + Social Sharing

Tier: **2** (new pricing tier + major feature: sharing, notifications, public-link claiming)
Pre-launch start: once `docs/gtm/production-launch-checklist.md` blockers are cleared
Budget: ~$0–500 (founder-led, organic-first)

## Strategic narrative (Raskin)

- **Old game**: your record collection lives in a spreadsheet, a Discogs wantlist, or your head. Sharing what you're hunting means a screenshot in a text thread.
- **New game**: your collection is a living thing you can show people — hunting lists your friends can actually see, the moment you share them.
- **Vehicle**: AudioFile — catalog your vinyl, share your wishlist to anyone, get notified when lists land in your inbox. Free to start; $5/mo Premium for serious diggers.

## Key messages

1. **"Your wantlist, shareable."** Share your hunting list to a friend by username — they get a notification and can browse it in-app.
2. **"Links that stick."** Any public share link can be saved into your own "Shared with me" inbox with one tap.
3. **"Built for serious diggers."** Premium ($5/mo) removes limits on collection size, wishlist, and sharing.

## Target audience

- Primary: vinyl collectors who buy/hunt regularly (Discogs users, r/vinyl, vinyl Instagram/TikTok)
- Secondary: casual collectors who want to share gift-hint lists (holiday angle)

## Timeline

**Week -2 (after prod checklist is green)**
- Record demo clip (phone screen record: share → notification → claim flow)
- Write blog post + 3 emails + 8–10 social posts
- Prep Product Hunt assets: tagline (<60 chars: "Catalog your vinyl. Share the hunt."), 5 screenshots, first-comment story
- Line up 20 first-hour supporters (friends, beta users, vinyl community contacts)
- Email existing users a teaser: "Next week: your wishlist gets social"

**Week -1**
- Submit Product Hunt draft, finalize supporter list
- Schedule social posts
- Sandbox-checkout smoke test passed on prod (checklist item 4)

**Launch day**
- 12:01 AM PST: Product Hunt live + first comment
- 6 AM: blog post + all social posts
- 7 AM: email #1 to all users
- All day: reply to every comment/mention within 30 min

**Week +1**
- Email #2: "in case you missed it" + signup count social proof
- Post launch-recap build-in-public thread
- Fix whatever the first 100 users trip on

**Week +2 to +4**
- Email #3: power-user story (someone who shared a hunting list)
- 1–2 follow-up posts ("How collectors use shared wishlists")
- Review funnel: visits → signups → shares → premium conversions; decide paid amplification ($100–300 boosts on the best post)

## Risks

- **Prod blockers slip** (Paddle approval, Coolify env): do not announce until the sandbox checkout smoke test passes on prod.
- **r/vinyl self-promo ban**: engage the community 2 weeks before posting; message mods first.
- **Free→paid backlash from early users**: VIP exemptions already protect existing known users; launch email should grandfather-tone it ("thanks for being early — you've got lifetime Premium" for VIPs).
